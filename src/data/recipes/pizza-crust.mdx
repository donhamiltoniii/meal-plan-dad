### Ingredients

- 10 oz Canned Chicken
- 1 oz grated parmesan cheese
- 1 large Egg

### Instructions

1. Drain chicken
1. Spread chicken on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper
1. Bake at 350 for 10 minutes to dry out the chicken
1. Remove and place in a mixing bowl
1. Increase heat of oven to 500 degrees.
1. Add cheese and egg and mix.
1. Pour mixture onto baking sheet lined with a parchment and spread thin. Placing parchment paper on top and using a rolling pin makes this easier.
1. Optional: With a spatula press the edges of the crust in to for a ridge for the crust. This is beneficial is youâ€™re using topping that may slide off(i.e. eggs).
1. Bake the crust for 8-10 minutes at 500 degrees.
1. Remove crust from oven. Add desired toppings and bake for another 6-10 minutes at 500 degrees.
