### Ingredients

- 8 Jalapeños, hollowed
- 8oz Cream Cheese
- 8oz shredded cheese of your choice
- 8 slices bacon

### Instructions

1. Cut a "T" into one side of your Jalapeños and scoop out the seeds.
1. In a bowl, mix cream cheese and shredded cheese until throughly mixed.
1. Preheat air frier for 10 minutes at 400°
1. Stuff each pepper with cheese mixture and wrap with one slice of bacon. Wrap the ends of the bacon underneath so they stay together.
1. Cook for 15 minutes at 350°
1. Eat them all in one sitting.
