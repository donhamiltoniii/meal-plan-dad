### Ingredients

- 3 heads broccoli
- 1 cup shredded cheddar
- 1 red onion
- 0.5 cup sliced almonds
- 4 slices bacon
- 2 tbsp chopped chives
- 1 cup mayonnaise
- 3 tbsp apple cider vinegar
- 1 tbsp dijon mustard

### Instructions

1. In a medium pot or saucepan, bring 6 cups of salted water to a boil. While waiting for the water to boil, prepare a large bowl with ice water.
1. Add broccoli florets to the boiling water and cook until tender, 1 to 2 minutes. Remove with a slotted spoon and place in the prepared bowl of ice water. When cool, drain florets in a colander.
1. In a medium bowl, whisk to combine dressing ingredients. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
1. Combine all salad ingredients in a large bowl and pour over dressing. Toss until ingredients are combined and fully coated in dressing. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
